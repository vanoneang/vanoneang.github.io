# IT Grammar

6 more tips to help with grammar accuracy:

  1. Learn from your mistakes.
  2. Accuracy isn't always a priority.
  3. Concentrate on(专注于) grammar you find most difficult.
  4. Take your time.
  5. Don't create a bad impression.
  6. Listen to/read English material.

# Present tenses

  • Use the Present Continuous to describe current trends(倾向).

E.g. The world is getting overpopulated(人口过剩的).

  • Use the Present Simple for statements that are always true(事实).

E.g. The sun rises in the east.
